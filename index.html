About Me

Michael Cumings is a new web developer living in rural Minnesota with his two children, who love playing video games with their father and hate any food he prepares for them (yes, peanut butter and jelly is tasty, but come on already).

He has some web coding experience from before CSS was in wide use and tags like <center> and <i> were still used to provide specific formatting in HTML even though he knew that specific directives for formatting text on the web was for super lame normies and that the people worth impressing browsed the web using lynx. He is aware that the world has gone in a different direction and is eager to learn about making pretty websites.

He hopes to have something more interesting and informative to put here later on.
